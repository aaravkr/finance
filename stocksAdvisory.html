Stock Advisory Swing

We provide our clients accurate and timely equity best stock tips which drive them to make maximum profit from the equity market. Our stock advisory winng is devided into 3 categories:

  • Short Term Plan
  • Medium Term Plan
  • Long Term Plan

Short Term Investment ( 1 to 2 months)

Profitable Investment calls for short term Investors who are keen to make profit instantly. Short term calls are based on the Fundamental & Technical analysis with little more focus on Technical analysis. We have an accomplished and experienced Research team. Short term investment calls are valid for duration of 3 days to 30 trading days. If the target or the stop loss is not reached the call is closed, usually it does not happen. (Note: its 30 trading days and not calendar dates)

  • Profitable investment calls for short term investments are given based on the fundamental & technical analysis by our research team.
  • Short term investment calls are for 3 to 30 trading days (calls close after every 30 trading days).
  • Depending on the market conditions, 12 to 15 short term investment calls will be given every month. And our passed track record demonstrates a success rate between 65 to 70 percent. In better market conditions, we even achieve success rate of 75 percent
  • Our short term calls gives reasonable returns, our stop losses are between 3 to 5 percent and returns are between 8 to 12 percent.
  • Well defined stop loss and target are given for every call. Clients get instant SMS, so that they can take instant action.
  • We give a unique service to our Client, they would receive an open call position sheet every week end through e-mail. This helps Investors reconcile and review their calls.

Medium Term Investment (3 to 6 months)

Profitable Investment calls for medium term investments are given based on the Rational Analysis theory (technical + fundamental). We recommend stocks with consistent growth from leading sectors. The investment calls under this service are for 1 month to 5 months duration. Investors can hold these stocks up to a maximum of five months to yield decent returns on their investments. Usually stop loss is about 6 to 8 percent and returns are between 15 to 25 percent. This service is a good wealth creating tools in the equity market. Our success rate is better in this service around 70 to 75 percent. In better market condition we even achieve a success of 80 percent, means out of every 10 stocks we recommend 7 to 8 stocks achieve their targets

  • Profitable investment calls for medium to long term investments are given based on the Rational Analysis theory (technical + fundamental).
  • The investment calls under this service are for 1 month to 5 months. Investors can hold these stocks up to a maximum of five months to get desired returns.
  • Depending on market conditions we recommend about 13 to 15 investments calls every month.
  • Well defined stop loss and target are given with every call. The stop loss and the targets are revised and altered to get the best retunes and SMS are sent to the clients accordingly.
  • We give a unique service to our Client, they would receive an open call position sheet every week end through e-mail. This helps Investors reconcile and review their calls.

Long Term Investment (more than 1year)

Multi-Bagger investment ideas (long term investment) is our all time favourite Investment service and this service is quite popular amongst our clients. These stocks are identified using both fundamental & technical analysis (rational analysis) with more focus on fundamental analysis. We carry out comprehensive research on the companies indentified from ethical businesses. Apart from number crunching (financials) we look into the governance & management of the companies. All the aspects are meticulously studied before we recommend. Once having recommended for buying we consistently monitor the development of the company. Analysed Quarterly report is sent to the client for their evaluation. This truly is a wealth creation idea- The Ethical way. And hence offers the best investment strategy.

  • Multi-bagger investment ideas for 18 months to 24 months time frame are given based on our comprehensive research and analysis.
  • 7 – 8 Multi-bagger investment ideas are provided to the client for the specified service period, i.e. 12 months.
  • Expected returns range from 70 to 80 percent. In some cases it may exceed 100 percent too (for a period of 18 to 24 months)
  • Full guidance regarding entry and exit on every call.
  • Any major news on the stock idea, corporate actions, corporate results, will be notified to the clients through e-mail
  • Quarterly Earnings reports on the Multi-bagger will be emailed to the clients.
  • Call shifting, if any, will be notified to the clients through emails.
  • Call track sheet will be sent to clients every month end through e-mail.
  • One more point goes in favour of the Multi- Bagger investors is Zero Capital gains tax.
